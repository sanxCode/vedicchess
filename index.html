About Pcoint

VEDICBOARDS designs games based on rich values and hidden secrets rooted deep in the ancient civilization like that of 'Indus valley' that has been of the highest spirit for mankind, as far as mentioned in Mahabharat’ and so near as, the age of enlightenment, The treasure has been continued to pass on thru games like ‘chaturanga’ (chess) and ‘moksha pat’ (snake and ladder). Balanced development to begin with and gradually overall development through complete sports opens doors for unlimited inner capabilities as well. Inspired by the rich values of vedic games we are initiating ‘project chaturanga park’-chess theme park! This is our first step towards this long journey! The first game we are launching is Archer Chess.

For 7 years old along with an advanced version for chess players. The first board we are launching is ‘mokshapata’ and the ‘yantra of 64’.
